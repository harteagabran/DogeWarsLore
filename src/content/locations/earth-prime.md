# Earth Prime

## Sypnosis

Earth Prime is the games equivalent of our world. While you do not travel to this location in the game it gets hinted in libraries as a mystical place where magic loses power the longer it stays there. Eventually the plot will involve figuring out a way to get back to this dimension as the knowledge to portal there seems to be well guarded.

## Points of Interests

### Jaddys Home

Throughout the game Jaddy will have flashbacks of his time with his human in Earth Prime. They will mostly happen whenever Jaddy is at his lowest.
