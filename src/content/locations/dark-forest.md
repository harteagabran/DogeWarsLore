# Dark Forest (To be Named Later)

## Sypnosis

The Dark Forest is the first area of the game where the player is taught how to fight, move around, and start earning attack cards

## Places of Interest

### Shrine to the Unknown God

An abandoned shrine that Jaddy stumbles into his first night. Jaddy sleeps here before he is awakened by the sounds of soem goblins chasing a small child. For the moment nothing special is coming to this place but might be used as a later story point.

### Witch's Hut

One of the final places you get to near the end of Chapter 1, the hut is owned by a Druid that likes to shows herself as a talking Cat once Jaddy gets there. The hut warps into different illusions that are used to test Jaddy's motives. This is also where the spell is done to try to locate Jaddy's human.
