# Chapter 1

## Act 1

[Screen Fades into a forest, bush at the center before it starts to shake. We see Jaddy walk out of it shake his head and start looking around. He looks scared and he starts barking aloud a couple times only to be returned by a moment of silence...]

(The player is able to move around and interact with the bush as well as a nearby spring which serves as a way to heal up, eventually the player will feel the need to travel the only open path available to proceed to the next area)\

### 1-1 Meeting the Goblins

[Jaddy walks down the path until he comes across a big rock, we hear footsteps coming down opposite his path. Jaddy decides to cower and hide behind the rock as we see two goblins making their way down.]

Note: as we see the goblins talk we see Jaddy shaking a bit behind his rock, implying he is fearful of the goblins

Goblin 1: Did ya tell the chief bout what I heard?

Goblin 2: Da chief doesn't need to be concerned about thing you only may have heard.

Goblin 1 (a bit annoyed): Could you pretend to be concerned at least? Didn't sound like any beast I know...

Goblin 2: Probably just the wind...

Goblin 1: The wind don't bark...

Goblin 2: What if it's food?

Goblin 1: What if it's something that eats goblins?

Goblin 2: Then we'll know to run.

Goblin 1: _sigh_

Goblin 2: Cmon I think it was this way

(Goblin 2 starts heading down)

Goblin 1 (under his breath): I was supposed to be asleep an hour ago...

[Once the pair of goblins makes their way out of frame the player is able to control Jaddy again, if they try to follow the the goblins Jaddy will visibly shake and walk back the other way. The only path forward is to continue where the goblins came from going.]

### 1-2 Battle Tutorial

[Jaddy reaches a new area where he sees some rustling in a nearby bush, he shakes a little but manages to bark at the shrub. A small snake appears and hisses at Jaddy and the player is entered into a battle with the snake]

(Player is taught about movement, charged attacks and regular attacks, establishing a small combo of using a charged attack to stagger and a regular attack to deal damage. After the first snake is defeated, 3 more will appear and player is allowed to fight them normally. Snakes will deal 1 damage to Jaddy and have a really long cooldown to attack. They will run away if Jaddy gets down to 1 HP)

[After the fight.]

Goblin (Far off): I think I heard it this way, cmon

[Jaddy starts running away to the next area]
