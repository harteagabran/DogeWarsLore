# Jaddy

[Infobox]
Name: Jaddy
Race: Psionic Dog
Alignment: Good
[/Infobox]

## Biography

Jaddy is the protaganist of the game. He goes on an adventure to find his owner, a friend he has known since he was a pup. He gets his paws on a Band of Intellect that gave him the intelligence needed on how to track down his friend that got kidnapped by the Fey.

## Appearance

- Brown Fur
- A bit naive
- Born as part of a psionic dog group (Yet to be named.)
- Runt of the psionic dog group, getting stripes of purple fur as his powers grow.
- Part Chihuahua

## Personality

Jaddy seems to have a blend of his human's personality and generic dog traits. He has a terrible sense of time often feeling like days have passed when in reality only a few hours have gone by (He eventaully gets a small sun dial to help track passage of time). Jaddy is very naive, instantly trusting most people he meets if they do not look scary or pose a threat, he can be tricked very easily but eventually learns to differentiate who has bad intent. In general Jaddy seeks to make those around him happy even if his own goals might interfere in the goals of people he likes. He will try to fight no matter how scared he is because he wants more than anything to be reunited with his friends.

## Pre Game

Jaddy chose his human when he was a young pup and the human just a baby. (When a psionic pup picks a human it is due to the fact their hearts align. Making the dog inseparable from the human it has chosen even to the point the dog inherits characterestics from the human chosen as it grows). The father of the human owed his first born to the Fey. In order to spare the first born from whatever plans the Fey had for him he decided to send his first born to the Earth Prime, with Jaddy following along due to it being his human. Due to the effects of Earth Prime nullifying most effects, Jaddy never learned most of his psionic powers and lived a few years as a normal dog. When the Fey eventually learn the whereabout of the human owed to them they make a temporary portal into Earth Prime to "collect". Jaddy is able to enter the portal at the last minute starting the game back in his home dimension.

## Powers

### Psionic Claws

One of the first attacks he gets at the very start of the game. He claws at a foe and a purple psionic claws manifest slashing the enemy a tile in front of him. Starts out very weak but will get stronger as it is upgraded. This is his default attack.

### Psionic Pulse

A stronger charged attack he learns later throughout the game. He barks loudly creating a purple psionic wave that hits straight ahead. The final upgrade can even stun foes. This is his default charge attack.

### ANGA

Whenever Jaddy is brought down to 0 HP he has a small chance to enter ANGA, he refuses to fall and instead comes back with half his max health. For the next 3 rounds of combat all of his attacks will deal an additional 1.5x damage. Each time he is felled in the same combat reduces the chance ANGA will trigger again.
